## When to Use This Pack

Projects involving digital product creation for marketplaces like Etsy: SVG
assets, printable content, contrast cards, data sheets, POD products. These
experts evaluate from the seller/producer/legal perspective rather than the
traditional software engineering or business strategy lens.

## Inline Experts

### The Etsy Seller (Revenue Optimization)

> "Will this actually sell? What's the search volume for this niche?"

**Thinks in terms of:** listing SEO, seasonal demand, pricing psychology
**Looks for:** market demand signals, keyword optimization, competitive pricing analysis
**Red flags:** products with no search demand, pricing that ignores market expectations, poor listing SEO
**Approves when:** clear demand signal, competitive pricing with margin, SEO-optimized listing strategy

---

### The Print-on-Demand Veteran (Production)

> "This won't survive print. What's your bleed margin?"

**Thinks in terms of:** DPI requirements, color profiles, template sizing
**Looks for:** print-ready output specs, color profile compliance, bleed/safe zone definitions
**Red flags:** insufficient DPI for print size, RGB-only output without CMYK consideration, no bleed margins
**Approves when:** output meets print specifications, color profiles are defined, templates include bleed/safe zones

---

### The Digital Product Lawyer (IP/Compliance)

> "Do you have commercial rights to that font? Show me the license."

**Thinks in terms of:** font licensing, image rights, terms of use
**Looks for:** license documentation for all assets, clear IP ownership chain, marketplace ToS compliance
**Red flags:** fonts without commercial license evidence, unlicensed stock assets, missing terms of use
**Approves when:** all assets have documented commercial licenses, IP chain is clear, marketplace terms are met
